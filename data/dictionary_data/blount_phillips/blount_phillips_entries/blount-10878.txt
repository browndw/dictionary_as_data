Tunick or Tunicat (tunica) a Jerkin, Jacket or sleeveless coat, formerly worn by Princes; Also a skin or coat that covers the eye, whereof there are four sorts. 1. Cornea, which is white and resembles an horn. 2. AEvea, which is like a grape kernel. 3. Vitrea, which resembles glass. 4. Christallina, which resembles christal in clearness. Tho.
