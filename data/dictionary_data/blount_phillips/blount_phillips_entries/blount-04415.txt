Fusil (Lat. fusillus) a little Spindle: It is also a term in Heraldry, and signifies that in a Coat of Arms, which is in a manner like a Spindle, as in that of Sire de Montagu (a French name) il porte dargert, trois fusillees en fasse de gueules. Min. The Fusil is longer then the Lozengea cent or Mascle, having its upper and lower part more acute, then the other two collateral middle parts. Gwill. fol. 358.
