Liverie, (French) the Cognizance, device, or badge, which a Noble-man, or Gentleman gives to his servants and followers, also Livery of seisin is a Ceremony used in Common-law, being a delivery of possession of Lands, or Tenements, or other things unto another, also a Writ, which lieth for the Heir to obtain the possession, and seizin of his Lands at the Kings hands.
