Niobe, the daughter of Tantalus and sister of Pelops, she was married to Amphion, and by him had 6 sons and 6 daughters, whom Juno prevailed with Apollo to kill with his Bow and Arrows, because their mother had adventured to preferre her self above the goddesse, and Niobe her self while she was railing against Juno was carried by a whirlewind into Asia, and there changed into a stone.
