Mainpernable (a Law word, and) signifies bailable, or that may be bailed; and Mainpernors, are the Sureties for one that is mainprized to appear at a certain day, &c. yet Mr. Manwood, 1. part. For. Laws, p. 167. makes a difference between Bail and Mainprize, viz. that he that is mainprised hath a greater liberty, then he that is bailed, &c.
