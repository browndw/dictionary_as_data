Statute (statutum) signifies a Decree or Act of Parliament, &c. 2. It is a kind of Bond; as Statute Merchant, and Statute Staple, An. 5. H. 4. cap. 12. The reason of which name is, because those Bonds are made according to the form of certain Statutes, which direct both before what persons and in what manner they ought to be made, West. par. 1. Symb. lib. 2. sect. 151.27. Ed. 3. ca. 9. and 23 Hen. 8.6.
