Glaucus, the son of Hippolochus, he assisted Priamus in the Trojan war, and taking Diomeds brazen arms for his own which were of Gold, he was killed by Ajaxa and his body being carried into Lycia by the winds, was changed into a River; Also the name of a fisher, who tasting of a certain herb, leapt into the Sea and was made a Sea-god.
