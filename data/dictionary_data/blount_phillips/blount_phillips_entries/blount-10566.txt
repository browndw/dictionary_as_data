Thraska cent es, are the followers of John Thraske, who broached his Judaical opinions in England about the year 1618. for which he was censured in the Star-chamber, but afterwards recanted. Mr. Full. Church hist. lib: 10. p. 76. There was also one Theophilus Braborn, a Disciple of his, who wrote a Book in defence of his heretical opinions, in the year 1632.
