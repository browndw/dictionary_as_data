Tabernacle (tabernaculum) a Pavillion or Tent for War; a little Shop, Shed, or Booth, made of bords or boughs. The Tabernacle of God you may read described, Exod. 26. It was made like a Tent to remove to and fro as occasion required, it had in it one room called Sanctum Sanctorum, wherein the high Priest entered but once a year, and another room called the Holy place, &c. Heb 9.2, 3, 4.
