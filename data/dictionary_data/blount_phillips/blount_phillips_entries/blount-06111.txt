Logick (logica) the Art of Logick, the Art of reasoning or disputing. Logick (according to my Lord Bacon) professes the preparation and contrivance of Aids and Forces for the understanding. Arts Logical or intellectual are four; Art of Enquiry or Invention; Art of Examination or Judgement; Art of Custody or Memory; and Art of Elocution or Tradition, &c. See his Advancement of Learning, fol. 218, 219.
