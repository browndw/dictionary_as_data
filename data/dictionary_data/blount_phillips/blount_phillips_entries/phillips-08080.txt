Muses, the 9 daughters of Jupiter and Mnemosyne, born in the Country of Pieria (whence they are called Pierides) and inhabiting Helicon a hill of Boeotia, they were accounted the goddesses of Musick and Poetry, and the rest of the ingenuous Arts and Sciences, their names were Calliope, Clio, Erato, Thalia, Melpomene, Terpsichore, Euterpe, Polyhymnia, and Vrania.
