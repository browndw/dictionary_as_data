Sybil (sybilla) a name of all women that had the spirit of Prophecy; there were ten famous, the first was called Persica, 2. Lybica, 3. Delphica, 4. Cumaea, 5. Erythraea, 6. Samia, 7. Cumana, 8. Hellespontica, 9. Phrygia, 10. Tiburtina. They all prophesied of Christs Incarnation. But the certain number of them is not agreed on among Authors, some making more, others fewer; whereof read Vulgar Errors, pag. 150.
