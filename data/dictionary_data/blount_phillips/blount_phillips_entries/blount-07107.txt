Obole (obolus) a Coyn, variable according to the Country, with us it is a half-penny: Junius takes it for a penny farthing of our money; others, for an smal Coyn worth seven pence. Also a half-penny weight, twelve grains among Apothecaries, and fourteen among Mintmen and Goldsmiths. Obolus terrae, five soot in breadth, and ten in length.
