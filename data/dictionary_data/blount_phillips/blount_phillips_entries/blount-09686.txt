Socina cent , a late Sect, begun by Laelius Socinus the Uncle, and made up by Faustus a cent ocinus of Sienna, his Nephew who first dispersed his Errors in Polonia, Transylvania, and Sarmatia, about the year 1555. They deny the eternal Divinity of the second Person of the Blessed Trinity, with other gross Errors. See Mr Chewney's Anti-Socinianism.
