Salmacian Salmacidan Spoiles (Salmacida Spolia) Spoils or Conquests got without blood or labour; from Salmacis a Fountain of Caria, which is said to enfeeble all such as either drank of it, or bathed in it; Hence that of Tully, Salmacida spolia sine sanguine & sudore, and there used for effeminate or venerial Conquests.
