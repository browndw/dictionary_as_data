Petty Treason (Fr: petit Trahison) Treason in a lesser or lower kinde. If a Servant kill his Master, a Wife her Husband, a Secular or Religious man his Prelate, these are Petit Treasons, An. 25. Edw. 3. cap. 2. Whereof see more in Stawnf. Pl. Cr. l. 1. c. 2. Cromp. J. of Peace f. 2. For the punishment of Petit Treason, see An. 22. H. 8. cap. 14.
