Raunger (from the Fr. Rang, i a rank, row, list, order) is an Officer of the Forest, but not within the Forest, having no charge of vert, but onely of Venison, that comes out of the Forest into his charge, or part of the Pourallee, to safe conduct them back again, &c. See Manw. part. 1. p. 50. and part. 2. c. 20.
