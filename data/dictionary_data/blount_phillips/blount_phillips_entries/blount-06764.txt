Mufti (i. an Oracle, or answerer of doubts) a chief Priest among the Turks (who is created by the Emperor) and hath four several orders or Sects subordinate to him (viz.) 1. The Calenderim. 2. The Haggiemlar. 3. The Torlocchi. 4. The Dervislar, &c. See Sir Hen. Blounts Voyage into the Levant, p. 79. See Brachman.
