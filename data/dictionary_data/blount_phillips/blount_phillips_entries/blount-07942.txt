Physia cent n (Physicus) The sence of this word in the common acceptation is wel known, yet we vulgarly abuse it, for a Leech or Medicus, but not altogether intolerably, because 'tis a trite and true saying, That ubi desinia cent Philosophus, incipit medicus, where the Naturalist (for there the word Philosopher stands for a Physiologer) ends, there the Medicus begins: So, as if an expert Leech, must needs be skil'd in the Physicks
