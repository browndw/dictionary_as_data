Ca cent raphy (chorographia) is a description of any whole Region, Kingdom or Nation; and is two fold; 1. Antient, by Tribes and Families, as Germany was divided betweee the Chatti, Cherusci, Suevi, Tencteri, &c. 2. Modern, into Shires and Provinces, as Germany now is into Francony, Saxony, Suevia, Bavaria, &c. Heyl.
