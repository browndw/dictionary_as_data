Melampus, the son of Amythaon and Dorippe, who laying him abroad in the Sun and covering all his body except his feet, they were so scorched by the Sun, that they became black, whence he was called Melampus, i. Blackfoot. He was a famous Physitian and understood the voices of birds and beasts, he cured the daughters of Praetus of their madnesse, one of whom named Iphianassa he married.
