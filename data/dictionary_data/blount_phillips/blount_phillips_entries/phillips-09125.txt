Petropolis, a Town in Northamptonshire, commonly called Peterborow, from a Monastery dedicated to St. Peter, begun by Penda, a Christian King of the Mercians & finisht by his brother Wolpher to expiate the crime of murdering his two sons Wolphald and Rufin. This Town was anciently called Medeswelhamsted or Medeshamsted, from Medeswell a deep whirlpool.
