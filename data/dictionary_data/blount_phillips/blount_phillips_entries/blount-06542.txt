Metaphor (metaphora, i. translatio) is the friendly and neighborly borrowing one word to express another, with more light and better note; or when a word is changed from his own signification into some other near unto it; as laetas segetes, luxuriantia prata, &c. Swords hungry of blood; Heads disinherited of their natural Seigniories, &c. This, of all other figures, is the most used and useful.
