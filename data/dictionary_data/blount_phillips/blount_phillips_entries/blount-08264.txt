Premunire (more properly Praemonere) when any man for an offence committed, shall incur a Praemunire, it is meant, he shall incur the same punishment, which is inflicted on those that transgress the Statute made Anno 16. Ric. 2. cap. 5. (commonly called the Statute of Praemunire) and is to be out of the Kings protection, to forfeit Lands and Goods, and to be imprisoned, &c. See more in Fullers Hist. p. 148.
