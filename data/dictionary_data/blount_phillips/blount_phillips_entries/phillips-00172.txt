Acrisius, the son of Abas King of Argos, and father of Danae Jove's Paramour; he, having heard that he was to be kill'd by the hand of him that should be born of her, shut her up in a brazen Towr, but Jupiter descending in a showr of gold through the lights lay with her and begat Perseus, who afterwards cut off the Gorgons head, and coming with it to Arga cent s changed Acrisius into a stone.
