Nisi prius, is a Writ Judicial, which lies in case where the Enquest is panelled and returned before the Justices of the Bank; the one party or the other making Petition, to have this Writ for the ease of the Country, &c. It is called Nisi prius, of these words contained in the same Nisi die Lunae apud talem locum prius venerint, &c. See Assize.
