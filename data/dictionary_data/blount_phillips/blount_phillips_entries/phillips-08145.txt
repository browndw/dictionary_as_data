Nauplius, the son of Neptune and Amymone the daughter of Danaus, he was King of Eubaea, and father of Palamedes, who being by the means of Vlisses stoned to death, Nauplius in revenge made a great fire upon the Mountain Caphareus, which the Greek Navy taking to be the light of some near Harbour, sailed so near, that they were cast away upon the rocks.
