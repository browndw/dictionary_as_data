Oblation (oblatio) an offering; an aid or Subsidy money. Oblations are thus defined in the Canon Law. Oblationes dicuntur quaecunque A piis fidelibusque Christianis offeruntur Deo & Ecca cent esiae, sive res soli, sive mobiles sint. Nee refert an legentur Testamento, an aliter donentur. Cap. Cler. 13. quaest. 2. See Duaren. de Sacr. Eccles. Minister. ac Benef. cap. 3.
