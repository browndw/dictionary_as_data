Philosophy (philosophia) the love or desire of wisdom; a deep knowledge in the nature of things; there are three different kinds of it. 1. Rational Philosophie, including Grammar, Logick, and Rhetorick; and this dives into the subtilty of disputations and discourse. 2. Natural Philosophie, searching into the obscurity of natures secrets, containing besides, Arithmetick, Musick, Geometry, and Astronomy. 3. Moral Philosophie, which consists in the knowledge and practise of civility and good behavior.
