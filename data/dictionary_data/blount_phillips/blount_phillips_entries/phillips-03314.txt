Cycnus, the son of Mars, who was kill'd in a Combate with Hercules, whom Mars to revenge his sons death resolved to fight with, but before they came to blows, Jupiter parted them with a clap of Thunder, there was another Cycnus, the son of Neptune, who being invulnerable fought with Achilles, and could not be killed, till Achilles throwing him upon the ground, kneel'd upon his neck and stiffled him.
