Portsale. Ann. 35 H. 8. cap. 7.1. sale of fish presently upon return in the Haven. Among the antient Romans, those things were rightly sold in Portsale, which were publickly sold Per Praeconem sub hasta. i. by the Cryer, under a Spear sticked up for that purpose, and some Magistrate making good the sale by delivery of the goods, which were sold to him, who would bid most for them. Sigonius.
