Castleward, Is an imposition laid upon such Subjects, as dwel within a certain compass of any Castle, towards the maintenance of such as watch and ward the Castle. Mag. Char. c. 20. & an. 32. H. 8. ca. 48. It is used sometimes for the very circuit it self; which is inhabited by such as are subject to this service, as in Stows Annals, p. 632.
