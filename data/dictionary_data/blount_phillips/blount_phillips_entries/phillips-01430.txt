Beauchamp, a name of great honour and eminency from the time of King Henry the second, especially since Cicily de Fortibus descended from the Earles de Ferrariis matcht into their Family of this name were anciently the Earles of Warwick, the Barons of Kidderminster, and of Powick. Of late ages, the Title of Vicount Beauchamp, hath been conferred upon the Family of Seimours. In old Records it is written de Bello Campo.
