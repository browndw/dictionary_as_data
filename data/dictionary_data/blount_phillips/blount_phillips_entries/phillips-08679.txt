Palatinate, the Country or chief Seat of a Count Palatine or Paladine, i. a Supream Officer in a Sovereign Princes Palace; but it is more especially taken for one of the Electors of the Roman Empire called the Palsgrave, or Prince Palatine of the Rhene; also certain Knights of this Island, in ancient times called Knights of the round Table, were called Paladines, also Palatine signifieth adjectively belonging to a Princes Palace.
