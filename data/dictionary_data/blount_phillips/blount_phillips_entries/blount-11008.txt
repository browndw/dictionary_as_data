Basilick vein (vena basilica) the liver vein, which issues from the second main ascendant branch of vena cava, and is divided into two branches, a deep and a superficial one; the later whereof being neer the inward process of the Arm, and very neer the skin, is divided into other two; (viz.) a less which runs into the head vein, and together with it makes the Median or middle vein, whilest the greater passes along by the elbow, to the hand, and there makes the Salvatella.
