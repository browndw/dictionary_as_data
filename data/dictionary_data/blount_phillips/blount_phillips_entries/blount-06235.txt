Magick Art (magia) in general, is wisdom, or contemplation of heavenly Sciences, and is twofold; Natural, which is lawful, and is the ground of all true Physick, and the occult wisdom of nature, without which all mans Reason and Knowledge is Ignorance; The other is Diabolical, superstitious and unlawful, and is called Necromancy; whereby men attain to the knowledge of things by the assistance of evil spirits. Chym. Dict.
