Sequesa cent er Sequestiate (sequestro) to separate a thing in controversie, from the possession of both those that contend for it. And this is double, Voluntary sequestration or Judiciary. Voluntary is that which is used by the consent of both parties: Judiciary is that which the Judge of his Authority doth, whether the parties will or not. Cow. In what sence it hath been of late years used, very many know by sad experience.
