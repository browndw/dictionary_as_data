Crinisus, a River of Sicily, near to the City Segesta, of which it is related, that when the Trojan Virgins were to be exposed to the fury of a Monster, whom Neptune sent to destroy the Country because of Laomedons perfidiousnesse, Hippotes a noble Trojan committed his daughter Hegesta to the wide Sea, and she being by fortune carried into Sicily, Crinisus fell in love with her, and turning himself into a Bear, ravisht her, whereupon she became with child and brought forth Aca cent a cent tes, who was afterwards King of Sicily.
