Horatius Cocles, a famous Roman, who fighting against Pa cent rsenna, King of the Hetrurians, defended a bridge himself against all the enemies forces, untill the bridge was cut down, and after that he leapt into Tyber, and swomme to his own people, also Horatius Flaccus, a famous Lyrick Poet of Venusium, who was in high favour with Augustus and Mecaenas: whence Horatio is a proper name frequent now adayes, derived as some think from the Greek a in non-Latin alphabet a, i. e. of good eye-sight.
