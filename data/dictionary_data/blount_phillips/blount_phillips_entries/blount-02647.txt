Cycloa cent s (cyclopes) an ancient and big-bona cent d sort of people, which had but one eye, and that in the midst of the forehead, inhabiting the Island of Sicily; The Poets called them Gyants, because they were people of a mighty stature, of which rank was the so much famoused Polyphemus, that with such humanity entertained AElysses and his companions.
