Laocoa cent a cent , the son of Priamus and Hecuba, and Priest to Apollo, he was the first that disswaded the Trojans from receiving the great horse into the walls, and strook his Spear so hard against it, that the sound of the armes was heard within; whereupon it hath been affirmed, that for his despising the gift of Minerva, there came immediately two great Serpents, and first devoured his two children, afterwards himself.
