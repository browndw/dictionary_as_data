Leander, a noble youth of Abydos, a Town in Asia, situated upon the shore of the Hellespont, he being in love with Hero, one of Venus Nuns, who dwelt in Sestos, a Town of the opposite bank, used to swim over to her in the night time, which having done several times without danger, at length in his passage over, he was orewhelmed by a storm, and drowned in the Hellespont.
