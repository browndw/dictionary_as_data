Labyrinth, (Greek) a Maze, or place made with so many turnings and windings that a man once entered in, cannot finde the way out, whereof the two most famous were, that built by Miris King of Egypt, and that which Daedalus built for Minos, King of Crete, it is also by Metaphor used for any kinde of intanglement, or intricate businesse.
