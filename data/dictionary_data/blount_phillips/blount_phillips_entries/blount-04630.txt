Granado (Span.) a Pomegranate, an Apple filled with delicious grains; there is also a warlike Engin that being filled with Gunpowder and other materials is wont to be shot out of a wide mouthed peece of Ordnance, and is called a Granado, for the likeness it hath with the other Granado in fashion, and being fully stuffed as the other Granado is, though the materials are very different.
