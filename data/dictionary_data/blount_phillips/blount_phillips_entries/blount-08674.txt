RAbbi. About the time of our Saviours nativity titles began to be multiplied among the Jews, and among the rest those of Rab, Rabbi and Rabban were in special use; they are all derived from a in non-Latin alphabet aRabab, signifying multiplicatus fuit, and they sound as much as a in non-Latin alphabet a, that is, a Master or Doctor, eminently gifted with variety of knowledge. Moses and Aaron, p. 29.
