Logist, (Greek) one skilled in the Logistick Art, i. the Art of reckoning or casting account; also Logists were certain Officers among the Athenians ten in number, to whom all such as had ended their Magistracy gave an account within 30 dayes of all those affairs whereof they had had the administration, they also kept an account of the monies, and of all matters belonging to the publick Revenue.
