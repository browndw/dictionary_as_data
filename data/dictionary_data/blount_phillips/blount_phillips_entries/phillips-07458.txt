Mahumetanisme, the Religion and Law of the Turks, founded by Mahomet the first Emperour of the Saracens, there was also of this name a great Emperour of the Turks, who overthrew the Greek Empire, took twelve Kingdomes, and two hundred Cities from the Christians, whereof the chief were Constantinople, the Island Chalcis, Scodra, Trapezuntium, and Hydruntum, in Italie, but at the siedge of Belgrade, or as some say of Taurinum, he was overthrown, and put to flight.
