Iason, the son of Aeson, King of Thessaly, he was sent by his Uncle Pelias to Colchos, to fetch the Golden Fleece, and having overcome the Dragon, and Brazen-footed Bull, that were set to guard it, by the help of Medea, who was in love with him, he brought her away with him, and married her, but afterwards falling in love with Creusa, the daughter of Creon, he left Medea, who in anger burnt Creusa, and her Palace together.
