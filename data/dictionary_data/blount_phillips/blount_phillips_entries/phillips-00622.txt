Amphitryo, the son of Alceus Prince of Thebes, who married Alcmena, daughter of Electryon and Lysidice, upon that condition that he should revenge the death of her brothers upon the Teleboans and Taphians, but while he was in the War, Jupiter coming to her in the likenesse of her husband, and lying with her, she brought forth Twins, Hercules son to Jupiter, Iphiclus to Amphitryo.
