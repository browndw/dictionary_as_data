Spiritualities of a Bishop (spiritualia Episcopi) were those profits which he received as a Bishop, and not as a a Baron of the Parliament. Stwanf. pl. Cor. fol. 132. Such were the duties of his Visitation, his Benefit growing from ordering and instituting Priests, Pretestation money, that Subsidium charitativum, which upon reasonable cause he might require of his Clergy. Jo. Greg de Benefic. ca. 6. num. 9. And the benefit of his Jurisdiction.
