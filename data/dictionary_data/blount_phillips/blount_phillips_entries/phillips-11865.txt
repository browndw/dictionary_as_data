Verge, (French) a rod, wand, or Sergeants Mace; also the compasse about the Kings Court, that bounds the Jurisdiction of the Lord Steward of the Kings houshold, and of the Coroner of the Kings house, and is accounted twelve miles compasse; also a rod whereby one is admitted Tenant holding it in his hand, and swearing a cent a cent alty to the Lord of the Mannour, and for that cause is called Tenant by the Verge.
