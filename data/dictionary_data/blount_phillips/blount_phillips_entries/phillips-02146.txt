Catarractonium, or Caturactonium, the name of a Town near Richmond, anciently very famous, so called from a great waterfall near unto it, in the year 769. it was burnt by the Tyrant Beanred, but afterwards it flourish't again in the time of King Ethelred, who solemnised his marriage with King Offa's daughter in this Town, it is now called Catarrick-bridge.
