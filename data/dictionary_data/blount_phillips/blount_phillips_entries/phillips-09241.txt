Pirithous, the son of Ixion, he was join'd with Theseus in a perpetual league of friendship, and assisted him against the Centaurs that would have ravisht away Hippodamia, at last they going together to Hell to fetch away Proserpina, Pirithous was slain by Cerberus, and Theseus being taken prisoner by Dis, was freed by Hercules.
