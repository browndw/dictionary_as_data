Sorbonist are those learned Doctors and Batchellors of Divinity of the Colledge of Sorbon in Paris; which took denomination from one Robert de Sorbonne, who was one of the Almoners & Preachers of St Lewis the Ninth, King of France, and the Donor and Instituter of this Colledge, about the year 1264. 2 Part Tr.
