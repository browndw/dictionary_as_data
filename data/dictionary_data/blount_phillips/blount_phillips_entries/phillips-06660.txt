John, a proper name, signifying in Hebrew gracious, there have been divers great Kings, and other famous men of this name, as Kings of England, Scotland, Spain, and Portugal, 23 Popes of Rome, also Joannes Hircanus, a famous Captain of the Jews, who conquered Samaria, Idumaea, and a great part of Syria; Joannes Acutus, or Hackwood, an English-man, who performed several great martial exploits in Italy, under the Dukes of Milain; Joannes Guttenberg, a German, who invented the Art of printing, this name was anciently pronounced Juon.
