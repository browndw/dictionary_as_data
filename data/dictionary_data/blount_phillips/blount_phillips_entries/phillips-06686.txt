Iphianassa, Iphinoe, and Lysippe, the daughters of Proetus, King of Argos, who for despising the goddesse Juno, & equalling their own beauties to hers, were struck with such a Phrensie, that they imagined themselves transformed to Cowes, but Melampus being sent for, & offered for the cure of them half the Kingdom, and which of the daughters he lik't best, soon brought them to their former estate, and married Iphianassa.
