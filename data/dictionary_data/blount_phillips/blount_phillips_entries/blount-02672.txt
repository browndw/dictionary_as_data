Damage-fesa cent nt (a term in our Common Law) is when a strangers beasts are in another mans ground, without licence of the Tenant of the ground, and there feed, or otherwise spoil the corn, grass, woods, &c. In which case the Tenant whom they hurt, may therefore distrain and impound them as well in the night as in the day. But in other cases, as for Rent, and services, and such like, none may distrain in the night. New Terms of the Law.
