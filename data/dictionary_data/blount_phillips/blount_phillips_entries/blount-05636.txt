Invest (investire) to give or put into possession; Investitura propria dicitur quando hasta vel aliquod corporeum traditur A Domini; ut ait Feudista, lib. 2. tit. 2. with us we use likewise to admit the Tenant, by delivering a verge or rod into his hand, and ministring him an oath, which is called Investing: others define it thus, Investitura est alicujus in suum jus introductio.
