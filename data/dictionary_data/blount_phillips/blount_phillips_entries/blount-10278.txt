Synod (synodus) a meeting or Assembly of Ecclesiastical persons, for the cause of Religion. Of this there are four kinds: As first, a General Oecumenical or AEniversal Synod or Council, and that is, where Bishops, &c. of all Nations meet. Secondly, National Synod, where those of one onely Nation meet. Thirdly, Provincial Synod, where Ecclesiastical persons of one onely Province meet. Fourthly, Diocesan Synod, where those of but one Diocess meet. See Convocation.
