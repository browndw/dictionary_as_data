Sontick (sonticus) noysom, hurtfull. The Sontick disease (Morbus Sonticus) a continual and extream sickness, that hangs long upen a man, and is ended withina no certain time; and whereby we are disabled to perform our necessary business. The Falling Evil is such a disease. Sigonius infers, That every disease which hinders us in the performance of our businesses, is termed Sonticus.
