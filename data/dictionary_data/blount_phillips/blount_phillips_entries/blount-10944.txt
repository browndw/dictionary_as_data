Vald'ombre or Vala cent mbreux, a sort of Religious, begun by John Gualbert, a Florentine, about the year 1040. who betaking himself to the study of Religion, retired to a place in the Appennine, called Val ombreux or the shady vale; his followers wear a smoaky coloured habir, and follow the rule of Saint Bennet. Heyl.
