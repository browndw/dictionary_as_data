Brigidians, an order of religious persons instituted by Brigidia a Widow, Queen of Sweden, in the time of Pope AErbane the Fifth, about the year of our Lord 1372. it was as well of men as women, albeit they dwelt severally. Pol. & Heyl. The Nuns of this Order had a noble Convent at Sion in Middlesex, built by K. Henry the Fifth.
