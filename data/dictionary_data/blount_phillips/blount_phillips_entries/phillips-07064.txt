Learchus, the son of Athamas, King of Tha cent bes, and Ino, the daughter of Cadmus, he was slain by his father, who in a raging madnesse, took him for a Lyons whelp, whereupon Ino fled with her other son Melicertaa & both of them cast themselves into the Sea, and were changed into Sea gods, and called by the Greeks Leucothea & Paloemon, by the Latins Matuta & Portumnus.
