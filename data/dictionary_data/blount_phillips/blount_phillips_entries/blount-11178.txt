Vestals (Vestales Virgines) certain Virgins among the ancient Romans, consecrated to the Goddess Vesta, and therefore so named; they were alwayes chosen between six and seven years of age and continued thirty years in their Office; whereof the first ten years they bestowed in learning the cremonies of their Order; the second in execution thereof, and the last ten in teaching others; After it was lawful for them to marry: Their chief Office was to keep fire continually burning in a round Temple at Rome, in honor of Vesta; and if it chanced to go out, they were to renew it again with no ua cent ual fire, but such as they could get by art from the Sun-beams.
