Scene (scena) the front or forepart of a Theatre or Stage, or the partition between the Players Vestry, and the Stage; a Comedy or Tragedy, or the division of a Play into certain parts, viz. first into Acts, those again into Scenes, which somtimes fall out more, sometimes fewer in every Act; The definition of a a cent cene being mutatio personarum. In old time it signified a place covered with Boughs, or the room where the Players made them ready.
