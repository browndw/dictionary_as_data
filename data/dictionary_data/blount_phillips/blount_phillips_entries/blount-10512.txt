Thavies Inn in Holborn, is one of the eight Inns of Chancery, it was antiently the house of one John Thavie, an Armorer, of whom it was rented in Edward the Thirds time, by some Professors of the Law; but about Henry the Seventh's time it was purchased by the Benchers of Lincolns Inn, for the Students and other professors of the Law of Chancery, and still retains the name of the old owner, Mr. Thavie. The Arms of this house are Azure, two garbs in saltor Or, with a T. argent in a chief Sables.
