Metropolitan (metropolitanus) an Archbishop, so called because his Sea is in the Mother City of the Province. Of these we had two in England, viz. the Archbishop of Canterbury, and York. The stile of the first was (by the Popes decision) Metropolitanus & Primas totius Angliae; of the later, Metropolitanus & Primas Angliae. Fa cent llers Church hist. lib. 3. p. 39.
