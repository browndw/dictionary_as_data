Bellerophon, the son of Glaucus King of Epire, against whom Sthenobaea the wife of Praetus King of Argos conspired, because he refused her inticements; but he having overcome all difficulties, was commanded at last to kill the Chimaera, which he did with the help of Neptune, who sent him a flying horse called pegasus, which was afterwards plac't among the celestial Signs.
