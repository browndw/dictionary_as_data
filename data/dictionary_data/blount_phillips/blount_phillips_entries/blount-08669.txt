Quod-libet (Lat.) what pleaseth; a quirk or quiddity, In some Schools there are Quodlibets, or Quodlibetical Questions disputed pro and con, wherein a man may hold what part of the controversie he pleaseth; in which disputations, are many witty quips, jeers, jests, &c. for entertainment of the Auditors. Thence some may call a Qa cent ip, that pleaseth some, though it offend others, a Quodlibet.
