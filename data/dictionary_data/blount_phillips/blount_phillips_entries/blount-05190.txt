Impale (Fr. Empaler) to spit on a stake, to thrust a stake in at the Fundament and out at the mouth (a manner of death inflicted on offenders by the Turks) Also a term in Heraldry, signifying, as it were the halfing a Coat of Arms; when a Coat is quartered, the Escotcheon is divided into four parts; when impaled, into two.
