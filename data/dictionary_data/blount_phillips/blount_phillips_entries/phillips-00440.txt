Ala cent maeon, the son of Amphiaraus and Eriphite, he killed his mother for having betrayed Amphiaraus, and afterwards runne mad; but being cured by Phlegias, he married his daughter Olphaestbaea cent , giving her a Bracelet of his mothers, but afterwards falling in love with one of Achetous his daughters, called Callirhoe, he promised her the Bracelet which he had given his former wife, on condition she would marry him, but going to fetch it, he was slain by Temon and Axion, Alphesibaeas brothers.
