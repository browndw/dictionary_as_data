Membrane (membrana) the upmost thin skin in any part of the body, whereof there are many and of several appellations. As the Pleuretique membrane, which is large and two-fold, through whose doubles pass all the Sinews, Veins, and Arteries, which are between the ribs; the inside whereof (as also the brest or bulk) it wholly covers. Also a skin of parchment, or the pill or pilling between the bark and tree.
