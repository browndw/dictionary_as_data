Lara, the name of one of the Nimphs called Naiades, the daughter of the River Almon: she was delivered to Mercury to be carried to Hell for revealing to Juno the love of Jupiter to the Nimph Juturna the sister of Turnus; but Mercury falling in love with her by the way, lay with her, and begat twins called Lares or Household-gods, who are also named Penates.
