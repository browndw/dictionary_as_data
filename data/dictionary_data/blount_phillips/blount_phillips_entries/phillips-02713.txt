Common-pleas, is one of the Courts in Westminster, but in ancient time moveable. It was erected in Henry the thirds time, for the trying of all civil causes, both reall and personal. The cheif Judge whereof is called Lord cheif Justice of the Common-pleas: the rest of the Officers are Custos brevium; four Exigenters, fourteen Filazers, a Clark of the warrants, a Clark of the jurata writs, Clark of the Treasury, Clark of the Kings Silver, Clark of the Essoynes, and Clark of the Outlawries.
