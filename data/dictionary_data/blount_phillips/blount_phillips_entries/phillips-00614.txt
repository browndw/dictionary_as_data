Amphiaraus, the son of Oileus, he was a great Prophet, who was desired by Adrastus, to go to the War of Thebes, but he knowing he should not return, kept himself private, till being betrayed by his wife Eriphile, who was bribed with a golden bracelet, he was forced to go, but the first day he came to Thebes, he was swallowed up alive by the earth.
