Miletus, the chief Cia cent ie of Ionia, whose inhabitants the Milesii were accounted the potentest, and the richest people of all Asia; it was originally called Anactoria, now Melazzo, also a Citie of Caria, built by Miletus the son of Apollo and Arga cent a, or as some say, by Sarpendon the son of Jupiter, and the brother of Minos, and Rhadamanthus.
