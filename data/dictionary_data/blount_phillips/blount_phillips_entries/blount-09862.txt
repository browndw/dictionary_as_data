Stade (stadium) a race for men or horses to run in; also a proportion or measure of ground, called also a Furlong; whereof there are three sorts, viz. The Italian, containing one hundred twenty and five paces; the Olympick of one hundred and twenty paces; and the Pya cent hick of two hundred paces, all after five feet to the pace. Of these Stades, eight make an Italian mile, containing one thousand paces; see Furlong.
