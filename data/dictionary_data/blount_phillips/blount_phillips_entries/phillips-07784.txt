Meroe, an Island encompass't with the River Nilus, in which there is a Citie of the same name, built by Cambyses, whose sister was called Meroe, from wence the Citie and Island took their denomination, this Citie Astronomers make to be the farthest of the Northern Climats, whose parallel-line they call Dia Meroes, because it runs through the midst of the Citie.
