Vacancy Vacation (vacatio) a time of ceasing from labor or common business, leasure, exemption; Vacation hath a special signification in this Nation, being used for all that time respectively, which passeth between Term and Term at London, which every Almanack will tell you. And when such times begun and ended in our Ancestors days, See Roger Hovedens annals, parte posteriori. fol. 343 a. where you shall finde this intermission was called Pax Dei & Ecclesiae.
