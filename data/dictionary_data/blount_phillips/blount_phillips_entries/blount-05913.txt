Laudanum, Ladanuma or Labdanum (Lat. Ladanum) a sweet-smelling transparent Gum, gathered from the leaves of Cistus Ledon a shrub, of which they make Pomanders; it smels like wine mingled with Spices. It is hot and dry, and being annointed on the head with oyl of Myrtles, it strengthens the skin, and keeps hair from falling off.
