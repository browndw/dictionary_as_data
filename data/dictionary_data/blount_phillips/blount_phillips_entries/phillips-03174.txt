Creon, the son of Menatius King of Thebes, he was brother to Jocasta, the wife of Laius, and mother of Oedipus, who unfolded the riddle of Sphinx, and married Jocasta, not knowing her to be his mother, and by her had Eteocles and Polynices, who succeeded in the Kingdom, and were to reign by turns every other year, but they having killed one another in civil Wars, Creon regained the Kingdom, but behaving himself with much cruelty, he was overcome by Theseus, and slain.
