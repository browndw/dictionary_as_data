Venta Belgarum, the ancient name of Winchester, a pleasant Citie in Hantshire, called by the Brittains Caer Guente, by the Saxons Windanceaster, and by the vulgar Latins Wintonia; Venta giveth name also unto two other Towns, Caster in Northfolk, called Venta Icenorum, and Caerwent in Monmouthshire, called Venta Silurum.
