Ea cent na (Aetna) a hill in the Island of Sicily, which continually sends forth flames of fire, occasioned by the abundance of sulphur and brimston therein contained, which is blown by the wind driving in at the chaps of the earth, as by a pair of bellows, &c. of this hill there are many Poetical fictions which I omit: it a cent s now called Montgibal.
