Lotharius, the son of Ludovicus Pius, Emperour of Germany, and King of France, he succeeded his father in the Empire; but his brothers Charles and Lodowic, not contented with their portions, and raising an army against their brother, a great battle was fought at Fontenay, in the borders of France, where Lotharius was put to flight, but at last they came to an agreement, and Lotharius after he had reigned about 15 years betook himself to the Prumiensian Monastery. There were also several Kings of France and Dukes of Lorrain of his name.
