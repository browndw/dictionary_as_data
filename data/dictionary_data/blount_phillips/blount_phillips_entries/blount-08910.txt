Rea cent ievy (Plevina) is the bringing of the Writ called Repligiari facias, by him that has his Cattel or other goods distrained by another for any cause, and putting in surety to the Sheriff, that upon delivery of the thing distrained, he will prosecute the Action against the distrainer. It is also used for the bailing a man. Pl. Cor. fol. 72, 73, 74.
