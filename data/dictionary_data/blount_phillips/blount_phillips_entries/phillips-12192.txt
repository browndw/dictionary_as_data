Xenophon, the son of Gryllus a famous Athenian Philosopher and expert Captain, he went with an Army of 10000 men along with Cyrus into Persia, and after Cyrus was slain, brought back his Army with little losse through many strange Countries and divers great difficulties and dangers. He was for his Eloquence stiled the Attick Muse, and writ many choice and elegant books.
