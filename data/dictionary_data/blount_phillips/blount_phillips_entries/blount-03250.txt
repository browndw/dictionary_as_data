Dotkiy, A thing of small value, a kind of coyn (Stanf. pl. cor. fo. 37.) it seems to come of the Dutch word Duytkea cent , that is, the eighth part of a Styfer or French Shilling, of which Styfers ten in the Low Countries or ten Sols in France, are of the same value with an English shilling, viz. twelve pence.
