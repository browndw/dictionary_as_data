Scotists (scotistae) those that follow the opinions of Joannes Scotus, otherwise called John Duns, the subtile Doctor, who was born (as some contest) in Scotland, as others, in the North of England; he was a Minorite, very eminent in Scholastick Divinity, and died young at Colen in the year 1308. his followers are opposers of the Thomists.
