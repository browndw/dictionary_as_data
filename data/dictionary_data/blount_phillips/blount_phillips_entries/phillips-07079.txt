Leet, (Sax.) a Law-day whence Court Leet, is a Court, or Jurisdiction, containing the third part of a Sa cent ire, and comprehending three, or four wapen-takes, or hundreds, the Leets were called by the Romans Fasti, wherein the Praeta cent r might lawfully keep Court, and administer Justice, which was not done without the speaking of these three words, Do, Dia cent o, Addico, i. e. I give, viz. way to actions and suites. I speak, viz. the Law. I Judge, viz. matters and men.
