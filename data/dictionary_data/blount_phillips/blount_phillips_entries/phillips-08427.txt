Ochus, the sirname of Artaxerxes the son of Artaxerxes Mnemon by his daughter Ancestris, he subdued Aegypt, overcame Tennes King of Phoenicia, Hermias the Acarnensian Tyrant, Euagoras King of Cyprus, took Sidon, by the help of Mentor, he buried his Sister and Mother in Law alive, caused his Uncle with a hundred of his children and grandchildren to be slain, but at last was poisoned by his Physitian Bagoas.
