Controller, an officer who keepeth a Roll of other officers accounts. Controller of the Hamper, an officer in Chancery, who takes all things sealed from the Clark of the Hamper inclosed in leather bags, and takes a special charge of them. Controller of the pipe, an officer in the Exchequer, who writes summons to the Sheriffs to levie the debts of the pipe. Controller of the Pell, an officer of the Exchequer who keeps a controlment of the Pell of receipts and goings out.
