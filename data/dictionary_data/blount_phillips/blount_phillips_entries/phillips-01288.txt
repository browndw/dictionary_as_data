Baily, or Bailiffe, (French) a Magistrate appointed, within a Province, or praecinct, to execute Justice, to maintain the peace, and to preserve the people from wrongs and vexations, and is principal Deputy to the King, or Supream Lord, also the Officers of each hundred, and of Towns Corporate are called Bayliffs, there are also Bayliffs of husbandry belonging to private men, who are Lords of Mannours.
