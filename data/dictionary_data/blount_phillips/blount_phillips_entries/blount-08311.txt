Primer, a Prayer-book of the Romanists so called; containing the Office of the Virgin Mary; which is divided into seven several hours (as a memorial of the seven principal parts and Hours of our Saviors Passion) viz. the Mattins and Laudes; the Prime or first hour, the Third hour, the Sixth hour, the Nineth hour, for morning. The Evensong and Compline for the Evening; and these seven Hours are composed of Psalms chiefly, Hymns, Canticles, Antiphones, Versicles, Responsories and Prayers.
