Mia cent tima cent s (i. we send) is a warrant made to send an offender to prison. Also a precept sent by the King out of his Bench, to those that have the custody of Fines levied, that they send them by a day assigned by his Bench. West. par. 2. Symb. tit. Fines sect. 138. F. and 154. B. See also the Tables of the Regist. Orig.
