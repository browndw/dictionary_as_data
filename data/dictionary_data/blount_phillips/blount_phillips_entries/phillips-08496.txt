Omphale, a Queen of Lydia by whom Hercules was highly rewarded for killing a great Serpent upon the River Sangaris, and afterwards he falling in love with her and desiring to be intertained in her service, she compelled him to sit and spin, while she leaving her distaff, put on his armes and Lions skin; others say that he was sold to her by Mercury at the command of Jupiter, for having slain Iphitus the son of Euritus.
