Stremeworks, is a kinde of work in the Stanneries; Of these Mines and Tin-works (says Cam.) there are two kinds, the one called Lodeworks, the other Stremeworks: This lyes in lower grounds, when by trenching they follow the veins of Tin; that other is in higher places when they dig deep pits on the hills, which they call Shafts, &c. mentioned A. 27. H. 8. ca. 23.
