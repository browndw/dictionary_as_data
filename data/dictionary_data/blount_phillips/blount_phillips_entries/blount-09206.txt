Samaritans, people of Samaria, a Country and City of Syria. Also a Sect among the Jewes not allowed to commerce with them, nor to be Proselytes. They rejected all the Scripture, save onely the five Books of Moses. Denied the Resurrection; but held that there were Angels, &c. See Epiphan. Haeres. 9. & 14. and Moses and Aaron, p. 48.
