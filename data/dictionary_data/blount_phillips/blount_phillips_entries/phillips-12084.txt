Vulcan, the God of fire, the son of Jupiter and Juno, he was thrown out of heaven for his deformity, into the Isle of Lemnos, by which fall he became lame, he was brought up by Eurynome, the daughter of Oceanus and Thetis, he was the master of the Cyclops, and made Thunderbolts for Jupiter; also Hermione's bracelet, Ariadne's Crown, the Chariot of the Sun, the Armour of Achilles and Aeneas, &c. he would have married Minerva, but she refusing him, he married Venus, whom he having caught in bed with Mars, threw a Net over them, and exposed them to the view of all the gods.
