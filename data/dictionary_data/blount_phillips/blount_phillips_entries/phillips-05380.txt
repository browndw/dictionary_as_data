Grand Sergeanty, a certain kinde of service, whereby the Lords of Scrivelby, in Cheshire held their Land, which was to come well armed and mounted into the Kings presence upon the day of his Coronation, and by publick Proclamation to offer himself to maintain the Kings right by open Combat, against whosoever should dare to oppose it, this Tenure belonged by Hereditary succession to the Family of the Dimmocks.
