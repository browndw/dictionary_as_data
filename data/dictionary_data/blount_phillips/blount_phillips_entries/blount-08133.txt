Pratique (from the Span. practica) practise, or a thing done by practise; but among Merchants it is a Licence to Traffick; as in the Ports of Italy, and the Streights, if the Mr. of the ship brings a Bill of Health, that is, a Certificate that the place from whence he came is not infected with any contagious disease, then the Chief Magistrate or Signors of Health grant him Pratique, that is, leave to come in and Traffick.
