Terrar or Terrer (from terra) a particular or survey of a Mannor, or of ones whole estate of lands, containing the quantity of acres and boundaries thereof. In the Exchequer there is a Terrar of all the Glebelands in England, made about the II. of Edw. 3. Sometimes used for a suit-roll, or catalogue of all the tenants and Residents names within a Mannor, and what Lands they hold.
