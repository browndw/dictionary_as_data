Nestorians (so called from Nestorius, a German, their first Founder) a sort of Hereticks, that held (among other enormities) that in Christ were two Persons, as well as two Natures, &c. This Heresie was condemned by the General Council at Ephesus; And Nestorius his tongue rotted in his mouth, for having bin the Author of such Blasphemy; he lived in the days of Theodosius; & was by him made Bishop of Constantinople. Sands.
