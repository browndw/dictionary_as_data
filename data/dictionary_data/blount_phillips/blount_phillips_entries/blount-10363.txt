Tarshish the Ocean or main Sea, Psa. 48.9. Break the ships of Tarshish. Tarshish was the name of the Son of Javan, the son of Japeth, the son of Noah, Gen. 10.4. of whom Tarsus a City of Cicilia in Syria, had the name, Acts 21.34. from thence they went by shipping into far Countreys Africa, Judea, Ophir, &c. 1 Kings 22.48. and 10.21. Hereupon that sea was called Tarshish, and the name is generally applied to every Ocean. Wilson.
