Meleager, the son of Oeneus King of Calidonia, and Althaea, he gathered a company of valiant youths together to slay a wild Bore that wasted the Country of Aetolia, and having slain it, presented the head to Atalanta the daughter of Jasius King of Argos, which Plexippus and Toxa cent us the brothers of Althaea indeavouring to take away, he slew them both, and married Atalanta, but Althaea enraged at the death of her brothers, threw the brand into the fire which she had saved from the Destinies when he was borne, which as it burnt, he consumed away.
