Cefala, an Island of Africa, found out by the Portugals, in the year 1500. being three miles in length, and one in breadth. It hath two Villages and one strong Castle. In the year 1505 the Ilanders rebelled, but were quickly subdued by the Portugall's, who killed the King; they had chosen among themselves, and set up another King, as Deputy to the King of Portugal.
