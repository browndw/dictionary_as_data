Rape Wine (Fr. rapA(C)) a very small Wine, coming of water cast upon the mother of Grapes, which have been pressed; also the Wine which comes from a Vessel filled with whole and sound Grapes (divided from the cluster) and some Wine among, which being drawn out is supplied by the leavings of good Wine, put into the Vessel, and revived and kept in heart a whole year by the said Grapes.
