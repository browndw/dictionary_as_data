Ceres, the daughter of Saturn and Ops, by whom Jupiter had Proserpina, and being snatcht away by Pluto; Ceres wandered through the world to seek her, and came to the Court of Eleusius King of Attica, whose son Triptolomus she made immortal; and seating him in a Chariot drawn with winged Dragons through the air, she sent him to teach mortals the use of corn, whence she was adored as the Godesse of Agriculture.
