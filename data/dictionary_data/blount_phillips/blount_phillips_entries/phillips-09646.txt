Proteus, a Sea deity, the son of Oceanus and Thetys, he was reported to have been Neptune's Sheapheard, and the keeper of his Sea-calfs; the Poets also feign that he was a great Prophet, and that he could transform himself into what shape he pleased, Servius affirms that he reigned in the Carpathian Island, leaving Pallene, a Citie of Thessaly, where he first lived.
