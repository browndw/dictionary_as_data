Saucige (from the Fr. Saucisse) a kind of pudding, well known. The Bolona Saucige is made of Beef and lean Bacon in equal quantity, flayed and chopped small with half as much Lard, and some Pepper, Ginger and Salt; then put into a clean Ox-gut, half a foot long, and laid in salt for two days together, and after hung up in the smoak. Cot.
