Mausoleum (Lat.) a famous Sepulchre, built by Queen Artemesia in honor of her Husband Mausolus King of Caria, and accounted one of the Worlds Wonders, it being twenty five Cubits high, supported with thirty six curious Pillars, and consisting of 411 foot in circuit, all of Marble, and of famous sculpture, of which Martial, AA cent dentia MausolaeaLaudibus immodicis Cares ad astra ferunt.
