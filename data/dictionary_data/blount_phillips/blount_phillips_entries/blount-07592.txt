Pasquil or Pasquin, (from the Italian Pasquino) a Libel clapt on a Post or Image; so called from Pasquil or Pasquin, an old Statue or Image in Rome, whereon Libels, Detractions, and Satyrical Invectives are fixed, and on him fathered as their Author: There is also in Rome, another old Statue called Morforeo, whereon they affix answers to those Pasquils.
