Lais, a woman of Sicily, who going to dwell at Corinth, became a very famous strumpet, and exacted excessive rates for the prostitution of her body, afterwards removing to Thessaly, she was in such high request among the men of Tha cent ssaly, that the women out of envy killed her in the Temple of Venus, it was by her instigation that Alexander the Great caused Persepa cent lis to be burnt.
