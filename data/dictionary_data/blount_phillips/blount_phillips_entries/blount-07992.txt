Plumbagin (plumbago, inis) pure lead, turned almost into ashes by the vehemency of the fire; This is the artificial Plumbagin, and comes of lead put into a furnace with Gold or Silver Oar, to make them melt the sooner (by which employment it gains some part in the worth of those mettals;) There is also a natural or mineral Plumbagin, which (as Matthiolus thinks) is no other then Silver mingled with Lead, Stone, or Oar. Cot.
