Ninus, an ancient King of the Assyrians the son of Jupiter Belus, he very much enlarged the Assyrian Empire, overcame Barzanes King of the Armenians, Pharus King of the Medes, Zoroaster the King of the Bactrians, the first inventour of Magick, and Sabarius King of the Sagae, at length he was secretly made away by his wife Semiramis, (whom he had taken from Menon the Prefect of Syria) who conspiring against his life succeeded him in the Kingdom.
