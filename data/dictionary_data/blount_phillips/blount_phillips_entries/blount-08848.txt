Regrater (Fr Regrateur, i. a Huckster, or one that trims up old Wares for sale) in our Law did antiently signifie such as bought by the great, and sold by retail. Ann. 27. Ed. 3. Stat. 1. cap. 3. But now it signifies, him that buyes and sells any Wares or Victuals in the same Market or Fair, or within five miles thereof. Anno 5. Ed. 6. ca. 14. Ann. 5 Eliz. ca. 12. and 13 Eliz. cap. 25.
