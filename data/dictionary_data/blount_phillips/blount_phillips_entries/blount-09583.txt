Sherbet or Serbet (in the Persian Tongue, it signifies pleasant liquor) is a kinde of drink in great request, both in Turkie and Persia, and is compounded of juyce of Lemons, Sugar, Amber, and other ingredients; another sort of it is made of Violets, Honey, juyce of Raisons, and the like. Others say Sherbet is an Arabic word, and signifies drink in general.
