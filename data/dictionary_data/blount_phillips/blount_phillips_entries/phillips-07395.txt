Lydia, a Kingdom of Asia the lesse, so called from Lydus, the son of Atys, who perceiving the people grow too numerous for the Countrey, resolved to send out one of his sons, to whose lot it should fall, to plant a Colony in some other place, so that it falling to Tyrrenus his lot, he went out with a great multitude of Lydians, and choosing out a part of Italie, which lieth upon the Sea-side, he called it from his own name Tyrrhenum, his brother Lydus tarrying at home succeeded his father Atys in the Kingdom, and called it from his own name Lydia, whereas before it was called Maeonia.
