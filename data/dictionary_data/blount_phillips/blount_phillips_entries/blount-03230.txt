Dominicaa cent s, otherwise called Preaching or Black Friers, a religious Order instituted by St. Dominick a Spaniard, about the year 1206. he sent his Associates to preach the Gospel even to the furthest parts of the world then known, which they did with great success, as their Successors since have done, and do still even in India and America: This Order was confirmed by Pope Honorius the Third, about the year 1216.
