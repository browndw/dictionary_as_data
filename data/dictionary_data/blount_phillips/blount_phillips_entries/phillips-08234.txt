Nisus, a King of the Megarenses, against whom war was made by Minos, a King of Crete, to revenge the death of his sonne Androgeus, who was slain by the Megarenses and Athenians, conspiring together; at length Megara was taken by the treachery of Scylla, who for the love of Minos cut off her fathers purple Lock on which depended the fate of his life and Kingdom, whereupon Nisus pined away for grief, and was changed into a Hawk, and Scylla seeing her self despised of Minos pined away also, and was changed into a Partridge.
