Creusa, the daughter of Priamus and Hecuba, and wife of Aeneas, by whom he had Ascanius; she following her husband out of Troy when it was set on fire, was not minded by him till he came out of the City, by reason that he led his son in his hand, and carried his father on his shoulders through the flames, but going back to seek her, he could never hear what was become of her.
