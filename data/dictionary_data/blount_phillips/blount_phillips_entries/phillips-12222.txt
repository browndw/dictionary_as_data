Zeuxis, a famoua cent Painter of Greece, who contended with Timantes, Androcides, Ea cent pompus, and Parrhasius, all excellent Painters of his time; he painted a Boy carrying Grapes, the Grapes being done with so much life, that the Birds taking them for true Grapes, flew to them to peck at them, whereat he grew very angry at his own work, saying, That if the Boy had been drawn as well as the Grapes, they would not have peckt at them for fear of the Boy.
