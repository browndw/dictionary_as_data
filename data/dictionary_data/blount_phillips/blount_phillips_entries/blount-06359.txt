Maronites (so called from one Maron, who is mentioned in the fifth Act of the Constantinopolitan Council) a sort of Christians, onely found in Mount Libanus in Syria; they have a Patriarck, whose name is always Peter; they were a limb of the Jacobites; but received the Roman Catholick Religion in the Papacy of Clement. 8. who sent them a Catechism printed at Rome in the Arabian language. Sands.
