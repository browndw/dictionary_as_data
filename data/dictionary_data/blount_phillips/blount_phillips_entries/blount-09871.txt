Staple (Fr. Estape) a Mart or general Market, a publick Store-house in a Sea Town, or Town of Traffick, whither the Merchants of England, by common Order or commandment, did carry Wools, Woolfels, Cloaths, Lead, Tin, &c. (which are the Staple Commodies of this Land) for the utterance of them by the great.
