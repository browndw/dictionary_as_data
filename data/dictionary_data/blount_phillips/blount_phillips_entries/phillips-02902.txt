Conspiracy, (lat.) a plotting or secret consultation; but in Common law it is alwayes taken in the evil part, and signifieth an agreement of such as bind themselves by covenant or other allyance, that each of them shall assist the other malitiously to indite, or falsely to move and maintain pleas; also such as cause children within age to appeal men of felony, and such as receive men in the Countries, with liveries and fees to maintain their malitious enterprises.
