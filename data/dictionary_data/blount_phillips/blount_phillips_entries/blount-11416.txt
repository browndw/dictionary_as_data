Va cent as (Octava) is the eighth day following any Term or Feast; as the AEtas of Saint Michael, the AEtas of St. Hillary, the AEtas of St. Martin, the AEtas of St. John Baptist, and the AEtas of the Blessed Trinity, &c. as you may read An. 51. Hen. 3. Stat. concerning general days in the Bench. And any day between the Feast and the Eighth day, is said to be within the AEtas; the use of it is in the Return of Writs, as appears by the same Statute.
