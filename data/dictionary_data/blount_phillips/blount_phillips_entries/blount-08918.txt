Reprise Fr.) a taking back or again; But in our Law, Reprises signifie deductions, and duties, that are yearly paid out of a Mannor; as Rent-charges, Pensions, Annuities, Fees of Stewards or Bayliffs, &c. Hence we say the Mannor of A. yields so much clear yearly rent AEltra Reprisas, besides all Reprises.
