Priapismus, (lat.) a disease wherein there is an erection of the yard without lust, from Priapus the son of Bacchus and Venus, he being born at Lampsacus, became through the malice of Juno, who was his mothers Midwife, very ugly and deformed, yet he had something about him so pleasing to the women of Lampsacus that after he was banisht by the men of that place, they built a Temple to him where they were wont to sacrifice an asse, and called him the god of Gardens.
