Ino, the daughter of Cadmus and Harmonia, and nurse to Bacchus, she was second wife to Athamas, King of Thebes, and for causing Phryxus and Helle, whom he had by his first wife Nephela cent , to be banisht, she was punished by Juno, who possest her husband, with such a raving madnesse, that he took her for a lionesse, and forced her with her son Melicerta, to cast her self into the sea, where, by the compassion of the gods, they were changed into sea Deities.
