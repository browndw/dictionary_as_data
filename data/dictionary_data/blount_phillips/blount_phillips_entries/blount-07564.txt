Parole (Fr.) a word, a term; also a speech or saying. Lease Parol, that is Lease per Parol, a Lease by word of mouth, not written. It is also a term of War, when a prisoner is permitted to go at liberty for procuring another Soldier, prisoner with the enemy, to be exchanged for him, or for raising such a ransom by a day agreed on, or upon any other occasion or agreement; and in default, the prisoner gives his Parole, i. his word, to returna during which time, we say, the prisoner is upon his Parole.
