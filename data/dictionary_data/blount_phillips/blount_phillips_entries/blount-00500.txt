Anathem (from anathema with an epsilon or A breve) a man that is accursed or given to the Devil by Excommunication; also execration or excommunication it self. Anathema Maranatha is one accursed for ever, or eternal execration, 1 Cor. 16. Anathema belongs to all obstinate scandalous offenders, Anathema Maranatha, onely to blasphemers of the Holy Ghost. Gal. 19. Rom. 9.2.
