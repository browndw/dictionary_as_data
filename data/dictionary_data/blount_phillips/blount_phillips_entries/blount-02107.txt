Cola cent sse (colossus) a great Image or Statue made for the honor of any person, as in Rhodes there was one 70 Cubits high, made by Chares of Lindum in twelve years space, to the honor of the Sun. This Colossus was made in the Image of a man, the Thumb of which few men could fathom. The brass of this Statue was so much, that when Muani, General of Caliph Osman, united Rhodes to the Mahumetan Empire, it loaded 900 Camels. Heyl. We read also of a Statue of Nero which was a hundred foot high. But above all, that of Mercury made at Auvergne containing four hundred foot in height, and of inestimable value. 2. part Treasury.
