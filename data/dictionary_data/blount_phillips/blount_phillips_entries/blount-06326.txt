Mantile or Mantle (Lat. mantile, Br. Mantel) a kind of cloak which Souldiers in times past used in Winter, a long hanging Cloak; it is taken for a long robe, An. 24. H. 8. ca. 14. Now it is called a Leaguer-Cloak; and the Ladies have appropriated the name to a kind of cloak which they wear. Also a term in Herauldry, and was antiently a kind of Cloak or Mantle, encompassing the Coat armor, but now is that flourish which proceeds from the Wreath, and Helm, and descends externally on each side the Escotcheon.
