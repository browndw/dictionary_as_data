Mantua, a City of Italy, situate upon the River Po, and built by Ocnus, who called it so from his mothers name Manto a Theban Prophetesse, the daughter of Tiresias, she after the death of her father fleeing from the tyranny of Creon King of Thebes, went first into Asia and built the Temple of Apollo Clarius, afterwards she came into Italy, where being got with child by Tiberinus, she brought forth Ocnus.
