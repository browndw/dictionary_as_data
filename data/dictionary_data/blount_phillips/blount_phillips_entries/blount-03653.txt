That which they had common with other Nations, was the Epoch of the Victory of the Greeks, which took beginning from the first Victory which Seleucus had against Antigonus in An. Muna 3637. an account much used by the Jews, Chaldeans, Syrians, and other Nations of the East. But the Chaldeans also had their own Epoch, or Account apart, reckoning their time from the first year of Nabonasser (Salmanassar he is called in Scripture) which being 438 years before this of Seleucus, must fall in An. Mun. 3201.
