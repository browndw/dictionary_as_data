Isthme (isthmus) a neck, a narrow piece of land betwixt two Seas almost meeting; also the neck of the wesand Est angustia illa intermedia inter Peninsulam & Continentem, & veluti quaedam Cervix, quae A Continente, velut A corpore gracilescens Peninsulam cum Continente tanquam Caput cum reliquo corpore connectit. See Peninsula.
