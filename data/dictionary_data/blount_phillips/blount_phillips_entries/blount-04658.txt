Grecians (Graeci) are used by St. Paul to signifie all the Heathen people, and stand in opposition with Hebrews, in the General acception containing both the Grecists or dispersed Hebrews, and also those of Palestina; the Grecists were both by birth and religion Hebrews, standing in opposition with Hebrews, in the strict acception, taken for those of Palaestina.
