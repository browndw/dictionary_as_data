Paraphonalia, is used in our Law, but in the Civil it is Paraphernalia, which are those goods a Wife brings her Husband, over and besides her Dowry or Marriage-money; as, Furniture for her own Chamber, her own Apparel, and Jewels, if she be of quality; all which she must have, and not the Executors of the Husband, &c. Shep. Fa. Counc. 122.
