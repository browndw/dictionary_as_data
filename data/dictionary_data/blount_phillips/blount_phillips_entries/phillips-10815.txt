Spartacus, a Thracian gladiatour or swordplayer, who with Chrysus and Oenomaus, broke out of Capua, got together an Army of slaves, and overthrew Clodius, Glaber, Lentulus, and Cassius; thereby making himself very formidable to the Romans; at last he was put to flight by Cra cent ssus, but afterwards making head again, was vanquisht and slain.
