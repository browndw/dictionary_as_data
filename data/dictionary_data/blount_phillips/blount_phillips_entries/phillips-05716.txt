Hesperus, the son of Japetus, and brother of Atlas, who flying from his Countrey, went and inhabited in Italy, whence that Countrey came to be called Hespa cent ria, he had three daughters, Aegle, Arethusa, and Hesperethusa, called the Hesperides, who lived in the Hesperian Garden, whose Trees bare golden Apples, that were kept by a watchfull Dragon, whom Hercules slew; it is also faigned of Hesperus, that after his death he was changed into the Evening Star.
