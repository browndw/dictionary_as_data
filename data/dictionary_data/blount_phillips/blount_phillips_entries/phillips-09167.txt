Philonomia, the daughter of Nyctinus and Arcadia, she, as she went a hunting one day with Diana, was got with child by Mars, and after she had brought forth twins she cast them into the River Erymantha cent s, who being a little after found by the Shepherd Tyliphus sucking a she Woolf, they were brought up by Tyliphus, and being called Lycastus and Parrhasius, succeeded in the Kingdom of Arcadia.
