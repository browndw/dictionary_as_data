Amphion, the son of Jupiter and Anta cent ope, who being married to Lycus, and after vitiated by Epaphus, was imprisoned by Dirce Lycus's 2. wife, but being set at liberty by Jupiter, she fled to the Hill Cytheron, where she brought forth Twins, Zethus and Amphion, who to revenge their mothers injuries, tormented Dirce by tying her to a wilde Bull's tale, but Bacchus pitying her, changed her to a Fountain, Amphion became so rare a Musician, that he was said to build the Theban Walls, by playing upon Mercuries Harp.
