Cyrus, the son of Cambyses and Mandana, he was King of Persia, overthrew the Assyrian Monarchy, conquered all Asia, but was at length overcome and slain by Thomyris, Queen of Scythia, who causing his head to be cut off, and cast into a tub of bloud, cried out, now satisfie thy self with bloud, after which thou hast alwayes thirsted.
