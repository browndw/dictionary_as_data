Terre-tenant (i. tenens terram) is he, who hath the natural actual possession of the land, which we otherwise call the occupation, A. 39 Eliz. cap. 7. For example, a Lord of a Mannor hath a Freeholder, who lets out his free land to another, this third person is called the Terre-tenant. West. part. 2. Simb. tit. Fines sect. 137.
