Pace (passus) a pace in going, a step or stride; of these there are two sorts, Passus minor vel simplex the measure of two feet and a half; which is usually the distance from the toes of the fore-foot to the heels of the hinder foot. Passus major sive Geometricus, a Pace or fathom of five foot, and by this Pace, miles are measured. In some places they reckon three foot and an half to the Pace. Tho.
