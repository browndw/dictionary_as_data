Theft-boa cent e (from theft and boote, i compensatio) signifies the receiving of goods from a thief, to the end to favor and maintain him; the punishment whereof is ransome and imprisonment, and not loss of life and member. Stawnf. pl. Cor. lib. 1. cap. 43. And the Mirror of Justices lib. 2. cap. des peches criminels al suite del roy: And yet he there makes mention of a Record alledged which testified a Judgment of life and member given in this case.
