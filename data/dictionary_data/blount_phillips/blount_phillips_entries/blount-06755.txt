Mot (Fr.) Motto (Ital.) a word; But in a restrained sence it is properly taken for the Word or short sentence applied to an ImprA(C)se or Devise. See Devise. There are also Motto's belonging to the Coats of Arms of most of the Nobility and Gentry, which are seldom in the English tongue. Mot, is also a Note, which a Huntsman winds on his horn.
