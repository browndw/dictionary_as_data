Mediolanum, the chief Citie of that part of Italie, formerly called Gallia Cisalpina, it was first built by the Gauls, who as they were digging in the earth, finding a sow half covered with wool like a sheep, called the Citie Mediolanum, vulgarly Millain, and the whole Countrey afterwards being Conquered by the Lombards, was thence named Lombardy.
