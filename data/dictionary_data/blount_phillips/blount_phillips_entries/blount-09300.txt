Scandalum Magnaa cent um (Lat.) is the special name of a wrong done to any high personage of the Land, as Prelates, Dukes, Earls, Barons, and other Nobles; as also of the great Officers of the Realm, by false news, or Messages, whereby debates and discords betwixt them and the Commons, or any scandal to their persons may arise. An. 2 R. 2. c. 5.
