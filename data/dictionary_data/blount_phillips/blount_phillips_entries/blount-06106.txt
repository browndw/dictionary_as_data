Lodestone (magnes) a stone of the colour of rusty iron, which hath an admirable vertue not onely to draw iron to it self, but to make any iron on which it is rubbed, to draw iron also. This stone is found in the Indian Sea, and in the Country Trachonitis; and is of greatest use in Navigation; For by it Saylers find out the certain course of their Voyage, the needle in the Compass, tempered herewith, still standing directly towards the North and South. Read more of this stone in Dr. Br. Vul. Err. l. 2. c. 2.
